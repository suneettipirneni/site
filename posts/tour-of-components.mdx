# Math

## Inline Math

This is an example of inline math: $x^2 + y^2 = z^2$

## Block Math

$$
\begin{aligned}
  x^2 + y^2 &= z^2 \\
  x^2 + y^2 - z^2 &= 0 \\
  (x + y - z)(x - y + z) &= 0 \\
  (x + y - z) &= 0 \text{ or } (x - y + z) = 0 \\
  x + y &= z \text{ or } x - y = -z
\end{aligned}
$$
